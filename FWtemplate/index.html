ABOUT

Wildlife Trust of India (WTI) was formed in 1998 in response to the many crises confronting India’s wildlife and wild habitats.
A team of 3 members set out on a mission to save the environment.

Our Project

Our Depth Projects holistically address multiple conservation hurdles specific to an area through a multi-pronged approach, and embody more than one of our nine Big Ideas.

Visualisation

We are the most trusted wildlife conservation charity organization in India, dedicated to preserve and protect the natural world and its wild habitats.

Photography

We have the best photographers in the field of Photography to capture the true livings emotions of the wildlife.

Organization Development

Over the past 20 years, we have been working constantly to serve and protect the wildlife and nature. Along with the immense support from all our stakeholders.
